FAQ

Common questions answered

Do I need Canva to use Digital101 AI?
+
Canva is helpful but not required. Digital101 AI gives you a detailed, copy-paste ready design prompt that you can use in Canva or any other design tool. The AI does the planning — you do the designing.
Does Digital101 AI design the product for me?
+
It gives you the product plan, full blueprint, Canva prompt, listing copy, and launch plan. You then take those into Canva or a similar design tool to build the actual product. Think of it as your AI business partner that handles all the strategy so you can focus on creating.
Is there really a free trial? What happens after?
+
Yes — Starter and Pro plans both include a genuine 3-day free trial. You will NOT be charged during the trial. If you don't cancel before the trial ends, your subscription begins. You can cancel at any time from your account settings.
What is your refund policy?
+
Refunds are available within 7 days of your first charge if you've used fewer than 5 AI generations. After 7 days, no refunds will be issued. The Starter Kit ($17) is a digital download and is final sale — no refunds. See our full Refund Policy for details.
Can beginners use it?
+
Absolutely. Digital101 AI was built specifically for beginners. There's no tech confusion, no blank-page panic, and no experience required. The step-by-step tool order guides you from your very first idea all the way to a launched product.
Can Digital101 AI guarantee I'll make sales?
+
No — and we'll always be honest about that. Digital101 AI is an educational and planning tool. It helps you create and launch faster. Your results depend on your product quality, marketing effort, consistency, and market demand. Most people who try to sell digital products online without consistent effort earn little to nothing.
